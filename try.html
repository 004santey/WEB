School Facilities

Library

The school has a well-equipped library with a wide range of books, magazines, and reference materials. It provides a quiet and comfortable environment for students to study and conduct research.

Laboratories

We have state-of-the-art science and computer laboratories where students can engage in hands-on experiments and practical learning. The labs are equipped with modern equipment and tools to facilitate effective learning experiences.

Sports Facilities

Our school offers a variety of sports facilities, including a gymnasium, basketball court and volleyball Ground. These facilities promote physical fitness and provide opportunities for students to participate in various sports activities.

Auditorium

The auditorium is a multipurpose hall that serves as a venue for school events, assemblies, performances, and presentations. It is equipped with modern audiovisual systems and can accommodate a large audience.

Cafeteria

We have a spacious cafeteria that offers a variety of nutritious and delicious meals for students. It is a social hub where students can relax, enjoy their meals, and interact with their peers in a comfortable environment.
